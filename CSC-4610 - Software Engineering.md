# SCRUM
Roles:

**Product Owner**
- Business Requirements
- Customer Knowledge
- Prioritization

**Scrum Master**
- Facilitate stand-ups (literally stand up and say what you did for the last _ days and what are your blockers)

### Sprints
- Series of Iterations (cycles of development)
- Time Boxed Work Period
	- Short
		- Often Limited to 2 weeks
	- Whole work items

## Sprint Planning
Cycle of planning for the sprint
- Focus execution
- Minimize Surprises
- Higher quality code

Should occur very early in

### Stand Ups
Occur Daily
Should be short
- What did I work on yesterday
- What am I working on today
- What are my blockers

Can add some fun while protecting time. 
- Consider passing an object around

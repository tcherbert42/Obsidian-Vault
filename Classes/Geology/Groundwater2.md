# Groundwater pt. 2 (LAB)
- **Groundwater** is the water that occurs beneath the surface of the Earth in the Zone of Saturation.
- The top of the zone of saturation is referred to as the **Water Table**
- Groundwater originates from precipitation which percolates downward through the porous **Vadose Zone** until it reaches the water table.

Variations in the Water Table 
- Seasonal variation 
- Human activity (pumping, etc.)
